Which Map is Right...

The earth is a beautiful place that needs to be explored, but what good is exploring if we don't have a way to record our explorations. That is why maps were created, so that us humans can easily travel to places and not get lost on our way.

Maps have been around for thousands of years, but they only started to get really advanced a few decades ago. They have gotten so advanced that now we need different maps for different purposes. You might be wondering what the correct map is. Since there are so many maps, and there must be one correct one.. right? No. The truth is that there is no such thing as the correct or proper map. They all have different purposes. Even the one on Google Maps is quite inaccurate, but it is definitely sufficient for its purposes.
